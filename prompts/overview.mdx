## Tips for Success

  <div>
    <Tip>
    <h3>üéØ Be Specific</h3>
    <p><strong>Vague prompt</strong>: "Write an email"</p>
    <p><strong>Good prompt</strong>: "I'm the Marketing Manager at Annoushka. Write a 150-word email to VIP customers announcing our Summer Charms preview event..."</p>
    <p><strong>More detail = Better results</strong></p>
    </Tip>

    <h3>üè¢ Add Context</h3>
    <p>Always mention:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Your role at Annoushka</li>
      <li>Who you're writing for (audience)</li>
      <li>What you need (goal)</li>
      <li>Any constraints (length, tone, format)</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>üîÑ Iterate</h3>
    <p>First result not perfect? Ask AI to:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>"Make it shorter"</li>
      <li>"Use a warmer tone"</li>
      <li>"Add more emphasis on craftsmanship"</li>
      <li>"Give me 3 more options"</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>‚úÖ Review & Refine</h3>
    <ul>
      <li>Check for brand voice alignment</li>
      <li>Verify any facts or figures</li>
      <li>Add your personal touch</li>
      <li>Make sure it sounds like Annoushka</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>üíæ Save Your Favorites</h3>
    <p>When you create a great custom prompt, save it! Build your personal library of go-to prompts.</p>
